About

  • I finished my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Duke University in May 2017. I worked with Dr. Benjamin C. Lee in the Computer Architecture Group.

    • My thesis focuses on designing efficient resource allocation and scheduling policies for managing datacenter applications, targeting current hardware challenges, e.g., processor heterogeneity, large memory systems, many-core architecture.
