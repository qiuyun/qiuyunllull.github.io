About

I am currently a Software Engineer at Google, working with the Platforms Performance team at Google Sunnyvale. I finished my Ph.D. program in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Duke University in May 2017, advised by Dr. Benjamin C. Lee in the Computer Architecture Lab. My doctoral research focuses on datacenter resource management and performance modeling. Previously, I also worked at VMware VMware as a Performance Engineer for two years, on designing the next-generation software-defined datacenters.
